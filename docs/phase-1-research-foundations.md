## 📖 How to Read Research Papers

### The Strategic Reading Approach

#### First Pass (15 minutes)
1. **Read the title and abstract**
2. **Look at figures and captions**
3. **Read the conclusion**
4. **Skim the introduction**

**Goal:** Understand what the paper is about and if it's relevant

#### Second Pass (1 hour)
1. **Read introduction carefully**
2. **Study figures and tables in detail**
3. **Read related work section**
4. **Skim the method section**

**Goal:** Understand the approach and how it fits in the field

#### Third Pass (2-3 hours)
1. **Read the entire paper carefully**
2. **Take detailed notes**
3. **Look up unfamiliar terms**
4. **Try to understand the math (don't worry if you can't)**

**Goal:** Deep understanding for your literature review

### Paper Summary Template

Use this template for each paper:

```markdown
# Paper Summary: [Title]

## Basic Information
- **Authors:** 
- **Year:** 
- **Venue:** 
- **Paper Link:** 

## The Big Picture
- **Problem:** What problem does this solve?
- **Solution:** What's their approach?
- **Results:** What did they achieve?
- **Impact:** Why does this matter?

## Technical Details
- **Method:** How does it work?
- **Experiments:** How did they test it?
- **Datasets:** What data did they use?
- **Metrics:** How did they measure success?

## My Thoughts
- **Strengths:** What's good about this work?
- **Limitations:** What are the weaknesses?
- **Questions:** What don't I understand?
- **Connections:** How does this relate to other papers?
```

## 🆘 Getting Stuck? Common Issues & Solutions

### "Papers are too hard to understand"
| Problem | Solution |
|---|---|
| Too much jargon | Focus on the big picture first, look up terms later |
| Complex math | Skip math sections initially, focus on concepts |
| Long papers | Use the strategic reading approach |

### "Don't know what to write"
| Problem | Solution |
|---|---|
| Blank page syndrome | Use the paper summary template |
| Don't understand everything | Write what you do understand |
| Comparing papers is hard | Focus on problem/solution/results |

### "Feeling isolated"
| Problem | Solution |
|---|---|
| Learning alone | Share progress in Discord |
| No one to discuss with | Join paper discussion threads |
| Imposter syndrome | Remember everyone starts somewhere |
