📝 Publication Guidelines​

Our Open Access Policy​

All research published through Average Joes Lab follows these principles:

  • Fully Open Access: No paywalls, ever
  • Creative Commons Licensed: Free to share and adapt
  • Community Peer Review: Reviewed by our research community
  • Transparent Methodology: All methods and data openly shared
  • Reproducible Research: Code and datasets made available

How to Publish With Us​

  1. Join Our Community: Connect via Discord
  2. Propose Your Research: Share your research idea in our research channels
  3. Find Collaborators: Connect with other researchers interested in your topic
  4. Conduct Research: Follow our open methodology guidelines
  5. Community Review: Submit for peer review by community members
  6. Publish: We'll help you publish and share your findings

Quality Standards​

While we believe in democratizing research, we maintain high quality standards:

  • Rigorous Methodology: Sound research practices required
  • Peer Review: Community-based review process
  • Ethical Standards: All research must meet ethical guidelines
  • Reproducibility: Research must be reproducible by others
  • Clear Communication: Findings must be clearly communicated
